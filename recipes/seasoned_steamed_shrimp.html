Seasoned Steamed Shrimp

Description

These shrimp are great as a main dish or as an appetizer. Serve with rice, or your favorite pasta dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons seafood seasoning (such as Old BayÂ®)
  • 1 pound fresh large shrimp, de-veined with shells on

Steps:

  1. Bring water, vinegar, and seafood seasoning to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Add shrimp and stir
  2. Cover and cook until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 4 minutes. Drain
